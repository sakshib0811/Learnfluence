WATER'O WATER


EXPLANATION




This poem tells us about the importance of water in our lives and it's uses in our lives. We all quench our thirst with water and we are all alive because of water. Water has been there since the birth of planet earth and thus it carries so many exciting stories that would fill some many pages.




Water comes in various forms. It can be in the form of dew (tiny droplets that you see on leaves and windows during winter season). It can be in the form of snow as well as steam.




River flows while making a beautiful musical sound. And when the river water falls from a waterfall, that sound is also soothing for ears. The water swells in lakes and springs.




Life on Earth was brought because of water. Water is used for irrigation of fields and farmlands which gives life to the plants that grow on those grounds. But sometimes, water can bring disasters. During floods, many people lose their houses, they have to face shortage of food and other essentials. The poet calls it a misfortune associated with water.
